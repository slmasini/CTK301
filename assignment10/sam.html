Our Mission:

Ruff Life Adoption is a no-kill animal shelter in the Bloominton/Normal area. Our mission is to provide shelter and find homes for abandoned cats and dogs. We provide people the opportunity to transport and foster animals so that you are not limited by distance. Please Contact us if you would be willing to donate or volunteer your time to protecting and saving lives.

Why Adopt a Senior?

Seniors are more likely to be house trained.

Older pets are calm and have less energy, making them less maintenence compared to younger pets.

Older pets make instant companions becuase they have been waiting their whole lives for this.
